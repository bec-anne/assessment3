Privacy Policy

Hello there!

At Snazzy Paws Grooming, your trust means the world to us. We're all about looking after your pets and your personal information with the same care and love. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe.

What We Collect

When you use our website, we might collect a few small details like your name, email address, phone number, and your pet's details. (Usually just when you book an appointment or send us a message.)

Why We Collect It

We collect your information so we can:

  • Book your appointments easily
  • Stay in touch about your pet's grooming
  • Make your experience with us smooth and stress-free
We promise — we never sell, swap, or share your personal info with anyone else.

Cookies (the tech kind, not the treat kind )

Our website might use tiny files called cookies to help it work better and give you a nicer browsing experience. You can always choose to turn cookies off in your browser if you prefer.

How We Keep Your Info Safe

We take your privacy seriously. We do everything we can to keep your information safe and secure.

Changes

If anything ever changes with our privacy policy, we'll update it here. We'll always be open and honest with you.

Talk To Us

Got questions? You're always welcome to reach out through our Contact Us page — we're here for you (and your fur babies)!
